Symptoms of heat-related illness

The three main types of heat-related illness are heat cramps, heat exhaustion and heat stroke.

1
Heat Cramps
Symptoms include painful muscle cramps and spasms, particularly in the legs, as well as moist skin.
2
Heat Exhaustion
Usually begins when the body's tempurature reaches 100.4 degrees Fahrenheit. Symptoms include heat cramps as well as nausea, vomiting, diarreah, headache, fatigue, weakness and anxiety
3
Heat Stroke
Usually begins when the body's temperature reaches 104 degrees Fahrenheit. Symptoms include those of heat cramps and heat exhaustion, as well as warm skin, rapid heart rate, loss of appetite, confusion, agitation, lethargy, stupor, seizures, coma and possibly death
