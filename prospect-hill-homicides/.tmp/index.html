2016 homicides in Prospect Hill

In 2016, there were four homicides in Prospect Hill, but residents say they have also seen a significant increase in armed robberies and prostitution. In response to the crime, some residents are circulating a petition calling on the San Antonio Police Department and city leaders to place a police storefront substation on North Zarzamora Street, which they say is especially plagued by crime. Each circle on the map marks a Prospect Hill homicide.
