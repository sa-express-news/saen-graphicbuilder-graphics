Murder, aggravated assault fuel crime increase

Between 2015 and 2016, "part one offenses" increased 8 percent in San Antonio, fueled mostly by increases in murder and rape. This table shows the total number of incidents in each category for 2015 and 2016.

Type of offense 2015 2016 Percent change
Murder and non-negligent manslaughter 94 149 59%
Rape 1,049 1,190 13%
Robbery 1,986 2,232 12%
Aggravated assault 5,465 7,183 31%
Burglary 11,632 12,235 5%
Larceny, theft 55,803 58,318 5%
Motor vehicle theft 6,176 7,233 17%
Arson 261 254 -3%
Total 82,466 88,794 8%
  • Source: FBI Uniform Crime Reporting
